A Beautiful Mind

It’s really hard to imagine someone who suffered from schizophrenia and was able to control himself by purely using his rational mind. Based on the two brain systems mentioned in Kahneman’s book, it’s not hard to conclude that those unreal figures, illusions created by schizophrenia come from the System 1 and the rational mind Professor John Nash was using to conquer it was from the System 2. If you watched the movie from the beginning to the end and if you could still remember, you might notice that by the time when Professor Nash went on the stage and made his acceptance speech, he said

"It is only in the mysterious equations of love that any logical reason can be found."

Think again of the two brain systems theory, it just so naturally illustrates that love provides all the attention we need and it comes from the system 1. Then all these love that we have received generates energy and powers the system 2 and this is what we usually called the “analytical thinking ability” or "logical reasoning ability".

当你努力的把自己的感受与世界剥离，这种力量，就像一种强烈的撕扯，把自己的灵魂与思想活生生的撕碎，一次次的撕碎， 一次次的碾压。每次你隔绝的自己，都会形成一种负面力量不断的打击你的情感。久而久之，为了阻隔外界不断的击打与伤害， 自己的机体便形成了一种自我保护机制，决定不要在感受外界，不要在接受自己的感受。而这仅仅是一个可怕的开始。你以为你的 世界从此就可以这样风雨无阻、无所畏惧吗？你想的太简单了。不会的。。。你会变的迟钝、你会变的如行尸走肉一般，呆滞的 生活在这个世界上。你不明白周围发生的事情，也不会理解人们的行为。你与世界的链接，你的一切，在你决定将自己的情感与外界 隔绝的时候，已经被生生的斩断了。
